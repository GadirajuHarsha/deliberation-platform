# Community Policies & Preferences: The Kinyarwanda Language Resource Group

The following are the established policies and general preferences of our community regarding data governance:

1. **Open Access Guarantee:** All raw voice data contributed to our datasets must remain openly accessible to academic researchers without prohibitive licensing fees.
2. **Attribution is Mandatory:** Any commercial entity utilizing our data must publicly acknowledge the Kinyarwanda Language Resource Group as the source.
3. **Collective Benefit:** We strongly prioritize collective community benefits (e.g., funding language preservation programs, community infrastructure) over individual monetary compensation for contributors.
4. **Governing Structure:** The active contributors elect a 5-member steering committee annually that acts as the legal negotiating body for the dataset.

*Note to the Socratic Facilitator: Keep these established preferences in mind when challenging the user. If they suggest something that contradicts these established preferences, gently point out the conflict to see how they resolve it.*
